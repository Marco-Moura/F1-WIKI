The current F1 points system awards points to the top 10 finishers in each race: 1st (25 points), 2nd (18), 3rd (15), 4th (12), 5th (10), 6th (8), 7th (6), 8th (4), 9th (2), and 10th (1). An additional point is awarded for the fastest lap if the driver finishes in the top 10. In Sprint races, points are awarded to the top 8 finishers (8 for 1st down to 1 for 8th).
